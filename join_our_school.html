Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline? expand_more

The application deadline for the upcoming academic year is March 15th. We encourage early applications as places are limited.

Is financial aid available?expand_more

Yes, we offer a range of financial aid options, including scholarships and grants. Please visit our financial aid page for more information and to apply.

Can we tour the campus?expand_more

Absolutely! We welcome prospective families to tour our campus. You can book a tour through the form on this page or by contacting our admissions office directly.

What are the school's core values?expand_more

Our core values are excellence, integrity, community, and respect. We strive to instill these principles in all our students throughout their academic journey.
